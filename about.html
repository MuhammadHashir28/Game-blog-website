About the Game

Batman: Arkham Knight is a 2015 action-adventure game developed by Rocksteady Studios and published by Warner Bros. Interactive Entertainment. It brings the acclaimed Arkham trilogy to its conclusion with a larger Gotham, upgraded combat, expanded predator encounters, and the first fully playable Batmobile in the main series.

The game is presented from a third-person perspective and focuses on melee combat, stealth, gadgets, detective work, and high-speed vehicle gameplay. Side missions let players hunt major villains across the city while the main story escalates toward a psychological battle for Batman's identity.
